## How to Be Listed Here

Contribute. Then open a PR or comment on an existing issue and we'll add you.

What counts as contribution:
- Code, including drafts and proposals (issues with working examples count as much as merged PRs)
- Documentation, including clarifying language and translations
- Architecture review, threat modeling, security analysis
- Patterns and primitives ported from other projects
- Stewardship of a working group, sub-project, or cadence meeting
- Connecting Wild Garden to real-world deployments at credit unions, municipalities, nonprofits, or small businesses

We list people, not corporate affiliations. Your contribution is yours, regardless of who employs you.
